Game

What is this game about?

This is a retro-style arcade game where you defeat enemies by matching their arrow patterns. Use your arrow keys to input combos and defeat enemies before they reach you!

How Waves and Rounds Work:

  • Rounds: Each round consists of 3 waves of enemies
  • Waves: Waves get progressively harder with more enemies
  • Progression: Complete all 3 waves to advance to the next round
  • Difficulty: Enemy count increases and spawn rates get faster as you progress

Enemy Combos:

Each enemy has a specific arrow combo pattern. Match the exact sequence to defeat them and earn points!

Card System:

Cards provide powerful abilities and bonuses to help you in battle. Choose wisely from the cards offered to you!

  • Wave Cards: After each wave (except the final wave), you can choose from 3 cards
  • Round Cards: After completing all 3 waves in a round, you can choose from 2 cards
  • Card Types: Cards come in different types (Defense, Attack, Movement, Score, Skill)
  • Rarity System: Cards have different rarities (S, A, B) affecting their power

Scoring System:

Rack up points by defeating enemies and building combo streaks! Here's how the scoring works:

  • Base Score: Each enemy defeated gives you base points (20 points)
  • Combo Bonuses: Longer enemy combos give bonus points (5 points per arrow beyond 4)
  • Score Display: Your total score is shown prominently on the left side
  • Recent Points: See your last 5 score additions below the combo tracker

Multiplier System:

Build up multipliers by defeating enemies consecutively without mistakes!

  • Multiplier Growth: Each consecutive defeat increases your multiplier
  • Maximum Multiplier: Multiplier caps at 3.0x for balanced gameplay
  • Duration: Multiplier lasts for 4 seconds, extend by defeating more enemies
  • Visual Indicator: Multiplier number and progress bar show current status

Combo Tracker:

Track your consecutive enemy defeats to maximize your scoring potential!

  • Combo Counter: Shows current streak of consecutive defeats
  • High Score Tracking: Your best combo is saved automatically
  • Combo Breaking: Mistakes or taking damage breaks your combo streak
  • Strategy: Maintain combos to keep multiplier active and score high

Controls

How to Defeat Enemies:

Defeating enemies requires matching their arrow combo patterns exactly. Here's how to do it:

Arrow Input:

  • Arrow Keys: Use the arrow keys (↑ ↓ ← →) to input combos
  • Visual Feedback: Your input appears at the bottom of the screen
  • Combo Display: Watch the enemy's arrow pattern above them
  • Match Order: Input arrows in the exact same sequence as shown

Submitting Combos:

  • Submit Key: Press Enter to submit your combo
  • Clear Combo: Press Enter again to clear and start over
  • Backspace: Remove the last arrow if you make a mistake
  • Perfect Match: Exact match defeats the enemy instantly!

Tips for Success:

  • Memorize Patterns: Remember enemy combos for quick defeats
  • Stay Focused: Don't let enemies get too close while inputting
  • Practice Speed: Faster combo input = better survival rate

Cards

Card Types & Rarity System:

Cards are organized by type and rarity. Higher rarity cards (S > A > B) provide more powerful effects!

ATTACK CARDS

Consecutive Combo II

For every 3 consecutive combos, reduce all enemy combo lengths by 2.

Twin Telepathy

When defeating an enemy, 30% chance giving two enemies on the field to have the same combo.

Minimum Combo

When defeating an enemy, 20% chance of an enemy on the field to have their combo reduced to minimum combo length.

Consecutive Combo I

For every 3 consecutive combos, reduce all enemy combo lengths by 1.

Bystander Death

When defeating an enemy, 10% chance of killing a random enemy on the field.

Combo Reduction: Correct

When defeating an enemy, 40% chance of reducing an enemy's combo length by 1.

Combo Reduction: Incorrect

If you enter a wrong combo, 30% chance of reducing an enemy's combo length by 1.

Combo Reduction: Longest Combo

When defeating an enemy, 20% chance of reducing the enemy with the longest combo length by 2.

DEFENSE CARDS

Heal II

Heal by 1 heart for every 5 consecutive combos.

Health II

Increase Max Health by 2 hearts and heal to Max Health.

Dodge II

When you take damage, you have a 40% chance of not losing health.

Heal: Even

Heal by 2 hearts in Rounds of an even number.

Heal I

Heal by 1 heart at the end of each round.

Health I

Increase Max Health by 1.

Wound Pulse

When you take damage, repel enemies for 1 second.

Dodge I

When you take damage, slow enemies for 2 seconds.

Blight

When you take damage, you have a 20% chance of not losing health.

Shield

When an enemy is close to you, create a shield that lasts for 2 seconds. Cooldown: 10 seconds.

MOVEMENT CARDS

Dash

Dash towards specified direction.

Speed

Increase speed for 3 seconds.

I SAID JUMP

Jump towards the cursor's position.

Switch!

Switch positions with the furthest enemy.

SCORE CARDS

Double No Chance!

Multiplier Ceiling

Multiplier Duration

Increase multiplier duration from 4 to 5 seconds.

Free Score!

Set multiplier base to x1.7.

Multiplier Increase II

20% of getting a Flat 10,000 score at end of a round.

Score Insurance

When damaged, reduce score decrease by half.

Combo Insurance

When incorrectly entering a combo, 40% chance it does not remove the multiplier.

50% Chance of doubling score of an enemy. Doubles before multiplier is applied.

Combo Insurance

SKILL CARDS

Invincible

Become invulnerable from damage for 8 seconds. Cooldown: 25 seconds.

Shockwave

Reduce combo length of all enemies by 3. Cooldown: 30 seconds.

Time Freeze

Enemies freeze for 3 seconds. Cooldown: 25 seconds.

Disorient

Enemies become distraught and move in random directions for 4 seconds. Cooldown: 20 seconds.

Your Just Like Me!

Cardinal Direction

Change all enemies to have combos using only one cardinal direction. Cooldown: 60 seconds.
